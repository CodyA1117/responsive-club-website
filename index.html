About Us

The Central West End Run Club brings together runners of all levels every week to explore the beautiful streets of St. Louis. Whether you're training for a race or just starting out, you'll find motivation and friendship here.

Weekly Meetups

Join us every Tuesday and Saturday for group runs! Locations rotate through Forest Park, Euclid Ave, and the STL Riverfront.

Get Involved

Ready to lace up? Reach out and join our newsletter for updates on routes, gear, and more.
